How to Boil Rice

This is a sample text to demostrate x, y, z

  • Stove in a Pot (13 Mins)
  • Oven (35 Mins)
  • Microwave (12 Mins)

    • Stove

    1 Cup of Rice

    2 Cups of Water


    1. Wash the rice in a colander until the water runs clear.

    2. Bring the rice to a medium high heat

    3. Turn down to low heat, cover with lid and cook for 13 minutes, rest for 10 mins

    Oven

    1 Cup of Rice

    2 Cups of Water


    1. Cover with lid, oven 35 minutes at 180*c

    2. Rest the rice at then fluff

    Microwave

    1 Cup of Rice

    2 Cups of Boiling Water


    1. Mircrowave on high 12 minutes, no lid

    2. Remove, cover with the lid and rest the rice for 10 minutes
